## Working on

Deep in Palantir deployments with several enterprise clients. The work is less about the technology at this point and more about organizational change — getting people to trust data they didn't produce and workflows they didn't design. Hard problem. Worth solving.

Also building out 10x Partners' content and outreach infrastructure. We've been quiet for too long. Fixing that.

## Reading

**The Sovereign Individual** — Michael Rees Mogg & James Dale Davidson. Prescient in ways that are either exciting or alarming depending on the day.

**The Founders** — Jimmy Soni's biography of PayPal. Useful reminder of how much chaos and luck is involved in the things we call inevitable in retrospect.

## Thinking about

Whether most AI deployments are solving the wrong problem. Every organization I talk to wants AI to automate tasks. Very few are asking what they'd do differently if they had perfect information. Those are different questions with very different answers.

Also: the gap between what Palantir sells and what most buyers think they're buying. There's a real mismatch there. It matters.
